Our Team

Amy Mikula, DDS

Dr. Mikula is a Maryland native, who grew up in Montgomery County. She has lived in College Park and Baltimore City, and currently resides in Towson. After graduating from the University of Maryland, College Park in 1999, Dr. Mikula went on to pursue her career at the University of Maryland Dental School where she earned a D.D.S. in 2005.

She was a resident in the University of Maryland's Advanced Education in General Dentistry program from 2005-2006. Dr. Mikula entered private practice in 2006, and established Lightpoint Dental with her husband, Dr. Trevor Marshall, in 2009.

Their three-year-old daughter keeps her very busy, but she still finds time to watch Maryland college basketball and for outdoor family activities. She has had pet birds since childhood and is fascinated by all things avian.

Trevor Marshall, DDS

Dr. Marshall grew up mainly in Columbia, Maryland, but spent his middle school years in Harford County. After high school, he went to Virginia Tech where he earned a degree in architecture. After working several years designing and building custom homes, he decided dentistry would be a better career fit.

In 2004, Dr. Marshall graduated from the University of Maryland Dental School, where he met his future wife. He completed the University of Maryland's Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency in 2005, then entered private practice.

When he's not at work, Dr. Marshall likes spending time with his daughter, especially if she's helping him garden. His architecture background makes Dr. Marshall pretty handy at home improvement.

Lexi Meyers

After graduating from Broadneck High School in 2010, Lexi entered the Medix School of Towson where she received her dental assisting certificate. She is both X-ray certified and has expanded function capabilities enabling her to complete a variety of dental tasks. Lexi has assisting experience in periodontics, oral surgery, and with the special needs patient population. She has a good rapport with patients and makes them feel at ease during procedures.

Lexi was raised in Anne Arundel county, currently calling Annapolis her home. On her days off, she likes spending time outdoors and with her Maltipoo dog, Ozzy.

Teri Nilsen

A graduate of the dental hygiene program at the University of Maryland Dental School, Teri has a Bachelor of Science and more than 31 years experience as a registered dental hygienist. No stranger to the neighborhood, she has lived in Anne Arundel County since 1973.

She and her husband love to race sailboats and have competed in the popular Wednesday Night series racing their J/30 boat for over 24 years. What she enjoys most is working with people—knowing she can give them something to smile about and that she is making an impact in her community.
