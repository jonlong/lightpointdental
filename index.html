Welcome to Our Office

Lightpoint Dental is our General Dentistry practice. Patients can expect us to listen, give treatment options, and do conscientious work. Our staff is friendly, empathetic, and down to earth.

Each one of us have been on the receiving end of dental treatment, and take care to remember what it's like being the patient. Lightpoint Dental has a calming, low-pressure atmosphere. We are serious about our work, but don't take ourselves too seriously.

Get to know us here, and call today to make an appointment for your next dental visit.

How to Find Us

Directions

From Route 97: Merge onto Route 50 East/US-301N/Annapolis Bay Bridge. Keep right at the fork and merge onto Exit 22, Route 665 (Aris T. Allen Blvd). Make a left turn at the first light onto Chinquapin Round Road, then the next left onto Forest Drive. Turn left just before the stop sign into the Forest Office Park parking lot. Suite J, 1831 Forest Drive is the left-most unit in the front.

From Route 50 East or West: Take Exit 22, Route 665 (Aris T. Allen Blvd). Make a left turn at the first light onto Chinquapin Round Road, then the next left onto Forest Drive. Turn left just before the stop sign into the Forest Office Park parking lot. Suite J, 1831 Forest Drive is the left-most unit in the front.
